üí° Conditional Past Structure:

avere/essere (conditional) + past participle

With AVERE:

  • avrei mangiato = I would have eaten
  • avresti comprato = you would have bought
  • avrebbe fatto = he/she would have done
  • avremmo parlato = we would have spoken
  • avreste visto = you (pl) would have seen
  • avrebbero detto = they would have said

With ESSERE:

  • sarei andato/a = I would have gone
  • saresti venuto/a = you would have come
  • sarebbe partito/a = he/she would have left
  • saremmo stati/e = we would have been
  • sareste arrivati/e = you (pl) would have arrived
  • sarebbero usciti/e = they would have gone out

Modal Verbs (Should have, Could have):

  • avresti dovuto studiare = you should have studied
  • avrebbe potuto aiutare = he/she could have helped
  • avrebbero voluto venire = they would have wanted to come

Common use: Expressing regret, hypothetical past situations, or polite past requests.
