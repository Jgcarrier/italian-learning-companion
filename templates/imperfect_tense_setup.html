The imperfect tense is used for:

  • Habitual actions in the past: "Ogni giorno mangiavo la pasta" (Every day I used to eat pasta)
  • Ongoing actions: "Mentre studiavo, lui guardava la TV" (While I was studying, he was watching TV)
  • Descriptions in the past: "Era una bella giornata" (It was a beautiful day)
  • Age and time: "Avevo dieci anni" (I was ten years old)

Endings: -avo/-avo/-ava/-avamo/-avate/-avano (are), -evo/-evi/-eva/-evamo/-evate/-evano (ere/ire)
