Advanced Pronouns: Ci & Ne

Master these tricky Italian pronouns

About This Exercise

Practice using ci and ne pronouns.

CI - Multiple uses:

  • Place: Ci vado (I'm going there)
  • About it: Ci penso (I think about it)
  • In it: Ci credo (I believe in it)

NE - Multiple uses:

  • Of them: Ne voglio due (I want two of them)
  • About it: Ne parliamo (We talk about it)
  • From there: Ne vengo (I come from there)
