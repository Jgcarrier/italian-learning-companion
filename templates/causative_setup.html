Causative Constructions

Practice: fare + infinitive, lasciare + infinitive

About This Exercise

Practice causative constructions using fare and lasciare + infinitive.

  • Fare + infinitive: to have/make someone do something
  • Example: fare vedere (to show, lit. "make see")
  • Lasciare + infinitive: to let/allow someone do something
  • Example: lasciare andare (to let go)
