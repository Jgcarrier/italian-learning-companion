## Quick Reference

### Grammar Progression
- **A1**: Present tense, articles, basic sentence structure
- **A2**: Passato prossimo, reflexive verbs, modal verbs, prepositions
- **B1**: Imperfetto vs passato, indirect pronouns, conditional, intro subjunctive
- **B2**: Full subjunctive, trapassato, periodo ipotetico, passive voice

### Tense Progression
- **A1**: Presente
- **A2**: Presente, Passato Prossimo, Imperfetto (intro), Futuro (basic)
- **B1**: All A2 + Imperfetto (full), Conditional
- **B2**: All B1 + Subjunctive (present/past), Trapassato, Condizionale composto

### Activity Types by Level
- **A1**: Fill-in-blanks, matching, simple dialogues
- **A2**: Past tense drills, short readings, listening comprehension
- **B1**: Narrative writing, comparisons, opinion debates, error correction
- **B2**: Essays, news analysis, authentic radio, transformations, hypotheticals
